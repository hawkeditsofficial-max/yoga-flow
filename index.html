About Yoga

Yoga is a holistic practice that combines physical postures, breath control, and meditation. It improves flexibility, strength, balance, and mental clarity.

Popular Poses

Downward Dog

Stretches hamstrings and strengthens arms. Hold 30–60 seconds.

Warrior II

Builds leg strength and focus. Hold 30–45 seconds each side.

Tree Pose

Improves balance and concentration. Hold 20–30 seconds each side.
